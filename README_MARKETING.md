## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my data really private?**
A: Yes. Every bit of your financial data stays on your computer. We never see it, store it, or send it anywhere.

**Q: What if I have multiple bank accounts?**
A: Upload as many files as you want from all your accounts. Everything gets combined into one dashboard.

**Q: Can I edit transactions?**
A: Absolutely. Change categories, delete incorrect entries, or reorganize as needed. Your AI learns from your corrections.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: Nope. No login, no password, no account to manage. Just use it.

**Q: What banks do you support?**
A: Any bank that exports CSV files. That's pretty much all of them.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Financial Dashboard works great on mobile browsers. No app needed.

**Q: How often can I upload new data?**
A: As often as you want. Keep your data current by uploading your latest statements whenever you like.

**Q: Does this replace a professional accountant?**
A: It's great for personal finance management and budgeting. For tax planning or complex financial situations, always consult a professional.
